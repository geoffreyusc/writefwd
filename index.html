The idea
Most people don't have a writing problem. They have an editing problem. The internal critic fires the moment the first word appears — and everything stops. writefwd physically removes the critic from the room.

Set a timer — or don't
5, 10, 15, 30 minutes. Or write until you're empty. The session ends when you say so.
02
Write forward
Backspace is gone. Words fade behind you as you type. The only direction is forward.
